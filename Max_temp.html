Maximum Temperature Vs Latitude

As expected, the weather becomes significantly wammer as one approaches the equator(0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemispere tends to be warmer this time of the year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
